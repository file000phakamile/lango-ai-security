Before you can scan any prompts, please review and accept this consent notice.

What Lango scans: every prompt you submit to a supported AI chat site while this extension is active is sent to your organisation's Lango backend for scanning before it reaches the AI provider.

What gets redacted or blocked: sensitive information Lango detects (e.g. national IDs, bank account numbers, phone numbers, names, medical information) is replaced with a placeholder before your message is sent, or the message is blocked entirely if Lango isn't confident enough to redact it safely.

What gets logged: your organisation's compliance team can see which prompts were flagged, what was detected, and the decision made — never your raw, unredacted prompt text, which Lango does not store.
